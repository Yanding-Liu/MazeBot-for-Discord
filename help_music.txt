**Music Commands**
```python
---------Basic Commands---------
\join: lets the bot join your voice channel
\leave: makes the bot leave your voice channel

\play: plays a song/playlist from youtube/bilibili
\search: searches for a song on youtube

\pause: pauses the current song
\resume: resumes the current song
\skip: skips the current song (You can use skipto/skipall)
\delete <num>: deletes a song from the queue

\queue: shows the current queue
\current: shows the current song

---------Advanced Commands---------
\load History: loads previous unfinished songs of the server
\load Mine: loads a playlist from youtube (You saved to this bot)
\save <Playlist>: saves youtube playlist to this bot
```
